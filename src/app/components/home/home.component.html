About the center

Legae La Bana Early Learning Centre (The Centre) is a non-profit organisation based in the rural area of Kgabalatsane (Morolong Section). The organisation was registered in March 2019 in response to shortage of Early Childhood Development (ECD) in the newly established section of Kgabalatsane.

Our mission

Legae La Bana Early Learning Centre provide Holistic Care for learners through promoting quality of life.

Our vision

:To be a caring organisation and light of hope for the children and community of Kgabalatsane.

Our values

Responsibility

The Centre is responsible and determined to create and build better future for the children of Kgabalatsane and surrounding areas. Commitment is our huge responsibility.

Excellence

We as Legae La Bana E.L.C are committed to a continuous state of excellence throughout everything we do. We are passionate about early learning childhood and service excellence.

Education

We believe that early learning promotes positive leaning experiences and provide an opportunity to succeed in life.

Quality

Every child deserves excellence in all learning programmes and services to create a strong foundation in their lives and our community. Quality education is what we offer.

Integrity

We believe integrity is the foundation of our organisation and it promotes effective relationship and team work in the Centre.
